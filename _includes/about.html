About

The evolution of the project.

  • 2012

    Our Beginnings

    The project was planned and designed by Pablo under ULEAM`s terrestrial ecology line, back in 2012. Additional funding came from ULEAM and Senescyt in 2013.

  • August 2014

    Camera Trapping

    Since the second half of 2014 we have been working hard to follow a standardized protocol to monitor terrestrial vertebrates and to study their habitat use patterns.

  • May 2015

    Spatial Modelling

    We hope to finish the sampling by the first semester of 2015, to start the data analysis and spatial modelling phases.

  • August 2015

    Phase Two Expansion

    We want to expand our project to Mache-Chindul ecological reserve, another protected area in Manabi and to continue the monitoring program for several years.
